About the Founder

Chef Arjun Patel

Founder & Head Chef, BurgerHub

Chef Arjun Patel is a culinary innovator and the passionate founder behind BurgerHub. With over 15 years of experience delighting taste buds across India and abroad, Chef Patel brought his vision of a premium burger destination to life in Food City. His unique flair lies in combining traditional grilling techniques with locally-sourced, high-quality ingredients to create burger recipes that are both classic and extraordinary.

From humble beginnings as a teenager experimenting in his family’s kitchen, Arjun’s journey led him through renowned culinary schools and top restaurant kitchens around the world. Upon returning home, he was driven by the belief that a burger could be more than just fast food — it could be the centerpiece of a memorable dining experience. Under his guidance, BurgerHub is now famous for its gourmet burgers, vibrant atmosphere, and a menu that celebrates bold flavors.

When he isn’t crafting new recipes, Chef Arjun enjoys mentoring aspiring young chefs and is often spotted greeting guests and sharing his burger secrets with customers.

"For me, food is about bringing people together and creating moments of joy. Every burger at BurgerHub tells a story — of flavor, passion and tradition."
- Arjun Patel

Milestones: Winner of the Food City Burger Fest Gold Prize (2022), featured on “India’s Top Chefs” magazine, and community mentor for local culinary schools.
